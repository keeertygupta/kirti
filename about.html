About Our Academy

Nurturing Excellence, Inspiring Leadership

Apex Horizon Academy was founded on the belief that education should be transformative, inspiring students to achieve academic mastery while finding their voice and purpose.

Our Mission

To provide a rigorous, values-based education that prepares diverse students for college and global leadership. We cultivate critical thinkers who are innovative, resilient, and committed to local and global service.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution recognized globally for producing innovative thinkers, ethical leaders, and lifelong learners. We envision a community where every child's potential is unlocked to make a positive impact on society.

Our History

The Journey of Apex Horizon

How we grew from a small neighborhood program into a leading academic institution.

2008

Academy Foundations

Apex Horizon Academy was established with a single classroom building, 45 students, and 6 dedicated teachers, aiming to offer localized advanced tutoring and holistic studies.

2013

Horizon Valley Campus

Following an influx of applicants, we inaugurated our current Horizon Valley campus, adding secondary classrooms, standard science laboratories, and an arts studio.

2018

STEM Center Launch

Recognizing technological growth, we built the STEM Center. We added advanced AP programs, coding curricula, and state-of-the-art engineering maker spaces.

2024

Global Citizenship Initiative

We launched international student exchange programs and university partnership pipelines, cementing Apex Horizon as a recognized preparatory academy.
