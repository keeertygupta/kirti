STEM Pathway

STEM Academy

The Apex Horizon STEM Academy is designed for students seeking depth in science, engineering, and coding. Combining advanced lab work with team design competitions, this program prepares students for major university engineering tracks.

Students have access to 3D printers, computer labs equipped with CAD software, mechanical tools, and high-performance physics apparatus. Regular guests from biotechnology and robotics companies share active engineering methodologies.

Creativity Pathway

Visual & Performing Arts

Our Arts Program nurtures the creative capabilities of every child. From classical canvas painting and sculpture to symphonic orchestra and drama productions, students find their unique voices.

Apex Horizon hosts two annual art exhibitions and two full-scale theater productions (a classical play and a musical). Studio courses in pottery, graphic design, and music theory are available to students at all grade levels.

Athletic Pathway

Titan Athletics

Titan Athletics builds team chemistry, resilience, and personal physical development. Under the guidance of professional coaches, our varsity and junior-varsity teams compete at state championship levels.

Sports available include Soccer, Basketball, Track & Field, Swimming, Volleyball, and Cross-Country. We place strong emphasis on character leadership, academics, and athletic discipline.
