FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find fast answers to common questions about enrollment, timelines, and services.

What is the application deadline for the upcoming academic year?

+
Priority applications close on February 15th for the fall semester. Regular admissions roll on a space-available basis until June 1st. We recommend applying early, as class sizes are capped.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid packages?

+
Yes. Apex Horizon Academy offers both merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid. Families can apply for assistance via the admissions portal after submitting their application. Up to 20% of our student body receives aid.

Is there a school bus service available?

+
Yes. We run secure, air-conditioned school buses covering major neighborhoods within a 15-mile radius of the Horizon Valley campus. Bus fees are calculated per student and can be paid annually or semi-annually.

What extracurricular sports and programs do you offer?

+
We support 12 varsity and junior-varsity athletic teams (including soccer, basketball, swimming, and cross country). Additionally, we offer 35 student-led interest clubs covering robotics, speech & debate, model UN, mock trial, and theater.
