Process Flow

Step-by-Step Enrollment Guide

We aim to make our admissions process clear, supportive, and straightforward.

1

Online Inquiry

Fill out our online inquiry form on the contact page or request a brochure to speak with our admissions counselors.

2

Application

Submit the online application portal along with academic transcripts, recommendations, and a small processing fee.

3

Assessment

Prospective students attend a campus tour, take an academic aptitude assessment, and join a short interview with faculty.

4

Enrollment

Upon review, acceptance letters are sent. Families confirm their student's slot by submitting enrollment paperwork and deposits.
