Payment Information

Is my online payment secure?

Yes. Transactions are fully encrypted using SSL, satisfying global payment gateway standards. No credit card information is cached or stored locally.

When will my account update?

Balances are updated immediately. A receipt and voucher confirmation will be dispatched automatically via email.

Are credit card fees added?

No. The academy covers processing overheads. The balance displayed on your invoice screen is the final payment amount.
